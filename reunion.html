OH SPRING 94 2024 REUNION WEEKEND

▶ Reunion Feedback Summary

Overall Sentiment: The feedback indicates a positive reception of the reunion, with many expressing enjoyment and a desire to continue the tradition in the future.

Key Suggestions:

Frequency of Events:

Many attendees expressed interest in making the reunion an annual event, emphasizing a desire to reconnect regularly.

Catering and Food:

There were several comments suggesting a reduction in food to minimize waste. Suggestions included ordering less food or adjusting quantities for future events.

Future Trip Ideas:

Ideas for future gatherings included organizing trips to other U.S. states, such as destinations out west, or even international trips like Barbados.

Planning Considerations:

Some attendees suggested alternating the planning of events between odd and even numbers, which could provide a structured schedule for future gatherings.

Positive Feedback:

Numerous comments highlighted the event's success, describing it as "amazing," "phenomenal," and an "awesome weekend." Attendees appreciated the overall experience and expressed eagerness for the next gathering.

Appreciation for the sisterhood activities and the service project.
